Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visiting hours for patients?

Visiting hours are from 10 AM to 8 PM every day. Special arrangements can be made for critical care patients upon request.

How can I schedule an appointment with a specialist?

Appointments with specialists can be scheduled through our online booking system or by calling our appointment desk at (555) 123-4567.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans, including but not limited to Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. Please contact our billing department for a complete list.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, a valid ID, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or referral forms.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our patient portal. If you need assistance, please contact our medical records office.
